NutriCare

A fully deployed, two-way AI-powered nutrition care platform connecting patients and doctors — with ML-based macro prediction, real-time AI chat, medication tracking, and structured clinical oversight.

https://nutricare-six.vercel.app/ https://nutricare-2.onrender.com

Problem Statement

Modern nutrition platforms deliver generic dietary advice with no clinical grounding. Patients managing chronic conditions — diabetes, hypertension, heart disease — require continuous, supervised monitoring. Doctors lack scalable digital tools to oversee patient dietary compliance and health trajectories. NutriCare closes this gap by building a structured two-way portal: patients log health data and receive AI-powered guidance, while doctors supervise, annotate, and intervene — all within one platform.

What Was Built

Every feature is live and functional in production.

ML Nutrition Prediction

Random Forest model predicts personalised macro targets (protein, carbs, fat, calories) based on age, BMI, activity, goal, and chronic conditions.

✓ Live on Render

AI Chat Assistant

Streaming AI assistant via OpenRouter (NVIDIA Nemotron) acting as a clinical nutrition advisor. Responses stream token-by-token for low perceived latency.

✓ Streaming enabled

Doctor–Patient Flow

Patients browse registered doctors and send requests. Doctors accept or reject. Accepted doctors gain full read access to patient logs, predictions, and medications.

✓ RLS-secured

Workout Logging

Patients log workout type, duration, intensity, and notes. Data persists to Supabase and is visible to assigned doctors in the patient detail view.

✓ Supabase persisted

Medication Tracking

Patients log medications with dosage, frequency, time of day, purpose, and notes. Doctors view the full medication list in the patient detail panel.

✓ Doctor-visible

Auth & Role Security

Supabase Auth with role-based access (patient / doctor). Row Level Security policies enforce data isolation — patients cannot see other patients' data.

✓ RLS enforced
